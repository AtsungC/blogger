Strife at eLife: inside a journal's quest to upend science publishing

There is a new evolution in the scientific pressing system. The eLite, non-profit press, initiated the novel method. The manuscripts will not be rejected and instead be published with comments between editors and authors. The transformation should be taken cautiously. I see the benefits to the scientific community as the discussions will be open. This will be a learning material for young researchers.
